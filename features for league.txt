Here's a comprehensive feature list for a sports club membership management SaaS application:

## **1. Multi-Tenant Club Management (SaaS Core)**

**Club Onboarding & Configuration:**
- Self-service club registration with branding customization (logo, colors, domain)
- Club profile setup (name, address, contact, social links, operating hours)
- Multiple branch/location support per club
- Club hierarchy (parent organization → regional → local clubs)
- White-label options for premium tiers
- Custom subdomain or custom domain mapping
- Timezone and currency configuration
- Language/localization settings

**Subscription & Billing (SaaS):**
- Tiered pricing plans (Free, Starter, Pro, Enterprise)
- Usage-based billing (per member, per feature)
- Trial period management
- Plan upgrade/downgrade flows
- Invoice generation for clubs
- Payment gateway integration (Stripe, PayPal)
- Revenue analytics per club for platform admin
