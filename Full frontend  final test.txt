19. **Animation Principles for Backgrounds:**
    - **Subtlety is key:** Animations should be felt, not consciously noticed
    - **Slow & smooth:** 15-60 second loop durations for ambient effects
    - **Low contrast movement:** Background animations at 5-15% opacity change max
    - **Easing:** Use ease-in-out, cubic-bezier for organic movement
    - **Layering:** Combine 2-3 subtle effects for depth (gradient + particles + noise)
    - **Focal hierarchy:** Backgrounds never compete with content—they support it
    - **Performance:** Use CSS transforms/opacity, avoid layout-triggering properties
    - **GPU acceleration:** will-change: transform; for smooth 60fps
    - **Reduced motion:** Respect prefers-reduced-motion—provide static but still gradient alternatives
